Hot-and-Sour Soup

This is a classic Chinese soup that is savoury, spicy, and tangy.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • 2 cups of thinly sliced cabbage
  • 1/2 pound firm or extra-firm tofu, cut into matchsticks
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 cubes vegetable bouillon
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne
  • 3 tablespoons cold water
  • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  • Green onions, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring to a boil in a 3-quart soup pot:
    • 4 cups of water
  2. Stir in and simmer for 3 minutes:
    • 2 cups of thinly sliced cabbage
    • 1/2 pound firm or extra-firm tofu, cut into matchsticks
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
    • 2 cubes vegetable bouillon
    • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  3. Stir in:
    • 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
    • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne
  4. Bring the soup back to a boil. Stir together in a small bowl or measuring cup:
    • 3 tablespoons cold water
    • 2 tablespoons cornstarch
  5. Stir the cornstarch mixture into the soup and simmer for a few minutes until it is slightly thickened.
  6. Garnish each serving with:
    • Green onions, chopped
    • 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil (optional)
